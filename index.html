Good to Know

Questions, answered.

The things most people ask before booking. Don't see yours? Just message us on WhatsApp.

How far in advance should we book? +

For weddings, 2–3 months ahead is ideal, especially for peak season dates (April, December, and weekends in general). Portraits, pre-shoots and smaller events can usually be arranged with a couple of weeks' notice — but the earlier you reach out, the more flexible we can be with timing.

Do you require an advance payment to confirm a date? +

Yes — a booking advance secures your date on our calendar, with the balance settled on or before the day of the shoot. We'll walk you through the exact amount and payment options once we've discussed your package over WhatsApp or email.

Do you travel outside Kurunegala? +

Always. We shoot island-wide — Colombo, Kandy, Jaffna, Galle, wherever your day takes us — and we've flown out for clients overseas too. Travel costs outside Kurunegala are added based on distance, and we'll quote that upfront with no surprises later.

How long until we get our photos and films? +

You'll get a same-week sneak peek of your favorites so you don't have to wait to relive the day. Fully edited photo galleries and the cinematic highlight film typically follow within 3–4 weeks, depending on the season and how many shoots we have queued.

Can we reschedule if something comes up? +

Life happens — weather, venues, family emergencies. Let us know as early as possible and we'll do our best to move your date to a new one at no extra charge, subject to our availability. Advances paid are carried over to the rescheduled date.

Do you only shoot weddings, or also kids, portraits and events? +

Weddings are a big part of what we do, but far from all of it. Birthday and kids' photography, family portraits, graduations, homecomings, and commercial/brand shoots are all part of the studio's regular work — have a look through the "Kids," "Portraits," "Events" and "Commercial" tabs in our gallery above.
