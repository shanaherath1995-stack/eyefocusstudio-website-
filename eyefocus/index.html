Who We Are

Every frame, shot like cinema.

Eye Focus Studio is built around one idea: your story deserves more than a quick clip — it deserves cinematic videography and frames that feel like they belong on a big screen. From gimbal-smooth movement to color-graded films, every shoot is treated as a short film, not a recording.

Based in Kurunegala, our team travels across all nine provinces — and beyond, for clients flying us in from overseas. Whether it's a wedding in Colombo, a homecoming in Jaffna, or a destination shoot abroad, we bring the same cinematic eye and the same commitment to getting it right the first time.
